Should you switch your
Shopify theme?

Switching is expensive and risky. Staying on the wrong theme quietly caps your conversion. Most advice online wants to sell you a rebuild, this just gives you a straight answer based on your situation: stay put, upgrade in place, or migrate.

The quick background

Shopify themes come in three generations now: old Vintage themes (security fixes only, no new features), Online Store 2.0 (the modern standard, ~78% of stores), and the brand-new Horizon architecture from Summer '26. Where you sit, how customized you are, and how much you're fighting your theme all decide whether switching is worth the cost. Answer below.

1. What kind of theme are you on?
Shopify admin → Online Store → Themes. No "Add section" on every page usually means Vintage.
2. When was your theme last updated by its maker?
Check the theme's changelog or the developer's site.
3. How customized is your theme?
This is the single biggest factor in migration cost.
4. Are you fighting your theme with apps and custom code to get basic things done?
Patching missing layout/section features with apps is a classic "outgrown it" signal.
5. How does it feel on mobile?
Most of your traffic is mobile; a dated theme shows there first.
6. Are you planning a rebrand or major redesign soon?
If yes, that's the natural moment to switch, you rebuild once.
