What's happening, in plain English

Google ran broad core updates in March and May 2026 (more will come). They don't "penalize" you, they recalibrate what ranks. The losers were consistent: duplicated manufacturer copy, thin or keyword-stuffed pages, and stores with no brand story, reviews, or expertise. The winners were stores with original product content and strong trust signals (E-E-A-T). This checks which side you're on.
